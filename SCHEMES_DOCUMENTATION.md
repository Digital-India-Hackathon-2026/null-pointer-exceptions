# BenefitBridge - Schemes and Document Requirements Documentation

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the 20 government and private schemes integrated into the BenefitBridge platform. It details the categories covered, eligibility rules, required documents, and step-by-step application instructions for each scheme.

## Categories Overview

BenefitBridge groups schemes into categories based on user eligibility profiles:
- **Farmers (`farmer`)**: Focuses on income support, crop insurance, and agricultural credit.
- **Disabled Citizens (`disabled`)**: Rehabilitation support, disability assistance, and low-interest business loans.
- **Women & Widows (`woman` / `widow` / `women`)**: Financial grants, entrepreneurship support, savings, and pensions.
- **Students (`student`)**: Scholarships for school, college, and postgraduate courses (both government and private/CSR).
- **Business/Entrepreneurs (`entrepreneur`)**: Micro-credit facilities and startup/business loan programs.
- **Employees & Labor (`employee`)**: Accident insurance, welfare, and pension schemes for both organized and unorganized sectors.
- **Senior Citizens (`senior`)**: Guaranteed pension schemes and social welfare support.
- **Healthcare (`health`)**: Family health insurance and medical treatments cover.
- **Low-income families (`low-income`)**: Cross-cutting classification for below poverty line (BPL) or economically weaker sections (EWS).

## Complete Scheme Registry

### 1. PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi
- **Sector**: Government
- **Source**: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
- **Categories**: Farmer, Low-Income
- **Eligibility**:
  - Age: 18+
  - Occupation: Farmer
  - Landholding family
- **Benefit**: ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal installments (DBT)
- **Documents Required**:
  - Aadhaar Card
  - Land Records / Khatauni
  - Bank Passbook
- **Application Process**:
  1. Visit pmkisan.gov.in and click New Farmer Registration.
  2. Enter Aadhaar and land record details.
  3. Upload documents and submit.
  4. Track status using the registration number.
